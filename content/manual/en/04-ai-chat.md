# 4. AI Chat {#ai-chat}

This chapter is for smart bed users. AI Chat is a text-based assistant built into the app. It helps you understand bed features, walks you through how things work, handles basic issues, and can take you to the right page when needed.

### 4.1 What AI Can Help With Right Now {#ai-capabilities}

Think of AI Chat as an always-available smart bed assistant. The current version is best at handling these types of questions:

- What a feature is — like a mode, preset, or sensor function
- How to use a feature — how to unlock, set up, or access something
- Basic troubleshooting — bed won't move, remote not responding, and other common issues
- Recognizing clear control requests and taking you to the right control page
- Explaining where sleep data comes from and guiding you to submit feedback

If your question is outside the current scope, AI Chat may direct you to human support or a feedback form.

### 4.2 Open AI Chat {#enter-ai-chat}

AI entry points appear on the home screen and on metric detail pages.

1. On the home screen, tap Ask me at the bottom.

![Home screen Ask me entry](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-entry-homepage-ask-me.png)

2. On a metric detail page's daily view, tap Ask me.

![Metric detail Ask me entry](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-entry-metric-detail-ask-me.png)

3. Once inside, check out the suggested questions or existing recommendations.

### 4.3 Review AI Suggestions & Keep Asking {#ai-suggestions}

After opening AI Chat, start by looking at the suggestions, then decide if you want to ask more. When you do ask, try to be direct about what you want to do or what's going wrong.

1. Look at the AI suggestions on the current page.
2. If there are suggested questions, tap one to see the reply.
3. To ask your own question, just type it in.
4. Not sure how to phrase it? Just write the feature name, the issue you're seeing, or the action you want.

![AI suggestions and input](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-suggestions-overview.png)

For example, you can ask:

- What is Auto Anti-Snore Intervention?
- How do I unlock Child Lock?
- Why does my base not respond to my remote?
- Go to zero gravity.
- Why is my sleep duration incorrect?

### 4.4 How AI Helps You {#ai-help-process}

AI Chat responds differently depending on your question. Here's what you'll typically see:

- Ask how to do something — AI gives you step-by-step instructions.

![AI how-to example](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-help-how-to.png)

- Ask about a metric or related info — AI shows the relevant chart data.

![AI chart data example](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-help-data-chart.png)

- Express a clear action (like moving the bed to a preset) — AI may show a feature card and guide you to the Remote page.

![AI feature card example](/manual/chapter-04/ai-chat/ai-chat-help-remote-card.png)

### 4.5 When to Use Human Support {#human-support}

AI Chat handles common questions, but it's not a full diagnostic tool. If the issue isn't resolved, the page may offer a human support link or feedback form.

You're better off reaching out to human support or submitting a feedback form when:

- The issue persists after following AI's troubleshooting steps
- The bed keeps failing to connect, won't move, or the same problem keeps coming back
- You need to submit a specific sleep data issue for further investigation or calibration

### 4.6 If Something Doesn't Look Right {#ai-issues}

If AI Chat only gives generic responses, or the answer doesn't match your situation, check these first:

- Are you connected to a smart bed?
- Does the current page have available data?
- Did your question clearly mention the feature name, the issue, or what you want to do?

If it's still not helpful, check {{jump-link|8.6 AI Content or Real-Time Data Temporarily Unavailable|troubleshooting|ai-data-unavailable}} first, then decide whether to switch to human support or a feedback form.
