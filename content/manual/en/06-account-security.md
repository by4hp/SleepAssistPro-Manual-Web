# 6. Account & Security {#account-security}

This chapter covers common account actions after you're signed in. Change your nickname, units, height, weight, or language. Manage sleep data and sharing. Turn on FaceID sign-in. Change or recover your password. Switch accounts or sign out. If you're thinking about deleting your account, read this chapter first to understand what happens.

### 6.1 Edit Personal Info {#personal-info}

To update your nickname, units, height, weight, or language, go to Account & Security, then open Personal Information. These settings affect how your profile appears and how some data is displayed.

#### 6.1.1 Open Account & Security {#open-account-security-personal}

1. Go to Settings, then tap Account & Security.

{{image-row|Settings|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png|Account & Security|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/AccountSecurity.png}}

#### 6.1.2 Edit Your Personal Information {#edit-personal-information}

1. Tap Personal Information.

![Personal Information page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/personal-information-form.png)

2. Update what you need.
3. Tap Confirm at the bottom.

If you're changing both units and body measurements, adjust Units first — it makes height, weight, and similar fields easier to verify.

#### 6.1.3 Change Language {#edit-language}

Just want to switch the app language? That's also in Personal Information.

1. Tap Personal Information.

![Personal Information page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/personal-information-form.png)

2. Find Language.
3. Select the language you want.
4. Tap Confirm at the bottom.

### 6.2 Manage Sleep Data & Sharing {#data-sharing}

To delete sleep data, download it, or manage sharing, go to Account & Security, then open Data & Sharing Settings.

#### 6.2.1 Open Account & Security {#open-account-security-data-sharing}

1. Go to Settings, then tap Account & Security.

{{image-row|Settings|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png|Account & Security|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/AccountSecurity.png}}

#### 6.2.2 Open Data & Sharing Settings {#open-data-sharing-settings}

1. On the Account & Security page, tap Data & Sharing Settings.

![Data & Sharing Settings page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/data-sharing-settings-overview.png)

#### 6.2.3 Delete Sleep Data {#delete-sleep-data}

Want to remove sleep records from specific dates? Use Delete Sleep Data.

1. Tap Data & Sharing Settings.
2. Tap Delete Sleep Data and pick the dates to remove.

![Delete sleep data date picker](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/delete-sleep-data-calendar.png)

3. After selecting, confirm in the popup.

![Delete sleep data confirmation](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/delete-sleep-data-popup.png)

#### 6.2.4 Download Sleep Data {#download-sleep-data}

Want to keep your own copy of your sleep records? Here's how.

1. Tap Data & Sharing Settings.
2. Tap Download Sleep Data and follow the prompts.

![Download Sleep Data page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/date-download.png)

#### 6.2.5 View & Manage Sharing {#sharing-settings}

Want to see reports shared with you, or share your own? Here's where.

1. Tap Data & Sharing Settings.
2. Tap Sharing Settings.

You can view what's been shared with you and manage your own sharing.

![Sharing Settings page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/sharing-settings.png)

### 6.3 FaceID Sign-In {#faceid}

If your device supports FaceID, you can turn on Sign In With FaceID in Account & Security. Once enabled, you'll need to pass FaceID verification each time you sign in.

#### 6.3.1 Open Account & Security {#open-account-security-faceid}

1. Go to Settings, then tap Account & Security.

{{image-row|Settings|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png|Account & Security|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/AccountSecurity.png}}

#### 6.3.2 Turn On FaceID Sign-In {#enable-faceid}

1. Find Sign In With FaceID.
2. Turn it on.

![FaceID sign-in](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/faceid.png)

3. If the system asks for FaceID permission, allow it — or go to system settings to authorize.
4. Next time you sign in, FaceID verification comes first.

### 6.4 Change or Recover Your Password {#password}

Two scenarios here: if you remember your current password, change it in the app. If you forgot it, email recovery is faster.

#### 6.4.1 Open Account & Security {#open-account-security-password}

1. Go to Settings, then tap Account & Security.

{{image-row|Settings|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png|Account & Security|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/AccountSecurity.png}}

#### 6.4.2 You Remember Your Password {#reset-password-known}

Change it directly in Account & Security.

1. Tap Reset Your Password.

![Reset Your Password page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/reset-password-form.png)

2. Enter your current password in Current Password.
3. Enter the new password in New Password.
4. Enter it again in Confirm New Password.
5. Tap Reset Password.

The new password must be at least 6 characters. Use it next time you sign in.

#### 6.4.3 You Forgot Your Password {#reset-password-forgotten}

Don't keep trying old passwords — email recovery is faster.

1. Tap Reset via email at the bottom of the page.
2. Enter the verification code from your email.

![Password reset code page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/password-reset-code-entry-filled.png)

3. Set a new password on the Create a new password page.

![Create new password page](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/password-reset-new-password-form.png)

4. Done — you'll be taken back to the Sign In page. Sign in with your new password.

![Back to Sign In](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/password-reset-success-sign-in.png)

### 6.5 Before You Delete Your Account {#delete-account}

Deleting your account removes all data and signs you out. This usually can't be undone — make sure you truly don't need this account anymore.

#### 6.5.1 Open Account & Security {#open-account-security-delete-account}

1. Go to Settings, then tap Account & Security.

{{image-row|Settings|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png|Account & Security|/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/AccountSecurity.png}}

#### 6.5.2 Check These First {#delete-account-checklist}

Before confirming, consider:

- Want to keep your sleep data? Use Download Sleep Data first.
- Only want to delete some sleep data? Use Delete Sleep Data first.
- Just taking a break from the app? Signing out is usually better than deleting.
- Just need a new password or FaceID? Use Reset Your Password or Sign In With FaceID — no need to delete.

#### 6.5.3 Ready to Delete {#delete-account-confirm}

If you're sure, follow these steps:

1. Find Delete Your Account near the bottom of the page.
2. Read the on-screen message again to make sure it matches your expectations.

![Delete account confirmation](/manual/chapter-06/account-security/delete-account-confirmation.png)

3. All good? Follow the prompts to complete deletion.

### 6.6 Sign Out {#logout}

Need to switch accounts or stop being signed in? Sign out at the bottom of Settings.

1. Open Settings.
2. Scroll to the bottom.
3. Tap Log Out.
4. Follow the prompts to finish.

![Settings page overview](/manual/chapter-06/settings-home/settings-home-overview.png)

After signing out, you'll need to sign back in to use account features and smart bed data.
