# 3. Using the App {#using-app}

After the connection and first-time setup are complete, use this chapter to get familiar with the app's most common features. It walks you through the Home page, Sleep Report, Real-Time Data, Remote, and Sleep Schedule.

### 3.1 Sign In {#login}

If you are using a smart bed, sign in here to enter the Home page. Adjustable beds usually do not require account sign-in before use.

#### 3.1.1 Sign In with Email {#login-email}

1. Open the Sign In page.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Tap Sign In.

![Sign In page](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161252.png)

4. After sign-in succeeds, enter the Home page.

![Home page](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Homepage-Full.png)

#### 3.1.2 Sign In with Apple {#login-apple}

If you signed up with Apple, tap the Apple option on the Sign In page, then complete authorization by following the system prompts.

1. Tap Apple at the bottom of the Sign In page.

![Apple sign-in entry](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161306.png)

2. Complete Apple authorization on the system screen.

![Apple authorization page](/manual/chapter-03/login/sign-up-apple-authorization.png)

#### 3.1.3 Sign In with Google {#login-google}

If you signed up with Google, tap the Google option on the Sign In page, then complete authorization by following the system prompts.

1. Tap Google at the bottom of the Sign In page.

![Google sign-in entry](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161315.png)

2. Complete Google authorization on the system screen. (Image missing)

![Google authorization page](/manual/chapter-03/login/sign-in-google-authorization-placeholder.svg)

### 3.2 Smart Bed Home {#homepage-smart}

If you are connected to a smart bed, the Home page brings together the items you will use most often, such as Sleep Report, key metrics, quick actions, and settings.

If this is your first time opening the Home page, use the order below to get familiar with it quickly:

#### 3.2.1 Sleep Score {#homepage-score}

1. If the selected date already has sleep data, the top area shows your sleep score and summary.

![Smart bed Home page](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Homepage-Full.png)

2. The page refreshes automatically once when it opens. If you want the latest data after that, you can also tap the refresh button manually.

![Pull to refresh](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.14.12@2x.png)

3. If you want to view data from another date, tap the date and switch it through the date picker pop-up.

{{image-row|Date entry|/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.14.44@2x%201.png|Date picker|/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Calendar.png}}

#### 3.2.2 Your Highlights {#homepage-highlights}

4. In Your Highlights, you can quickly review key sleep metric cards and swipe left or right to switch between cards.

![Highlights cards](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x.png)

5. If you want to adjust what is shown, tap Edit on the right.

![Highlights edit entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%201.png)

6. The Home page shows the top three cards. Drag them to change the order, then tap the button in the upper-right corner to save.

![Highlights order edit](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.17.30@2x.png)

#### 3.2.3 Quick Actions {#homepage-quick-actions}

7. In Quick Actions, open common features such as Real-Time Data, Sleep Schedule, and other shortcuts.

![Quick Actions area](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%202.png)

8. If you want to adjust the order or content, tap Edit on the right.

![Quick Actions edit entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%203.png)

9. In the same way, the Home page shows the top four actions. Drag them to change the order, then tap the button in the upper-right corner to save.

![Quick Actions order edit](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.20.48@2x.png)

#### 3.2.4 Other {#homepage-other}

10. If you want a more detailed analysis, tap the sleep score area or pull it down to open Sleep Report.

![Sleep Report entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%204.png)

11. If you need to change account, notification, device, or other settings, tap Settings.

![Settings entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%205.png)

### 3.3 Adjustable Bed Home {#homepage-electric}

If you are using an adjustable bed, the Home page is mainly for quickly controlling common bed functions such as positions, massage, lighting, and quick actions. It usually does not show account features, Sleep Report, Real-Time Data, or AI Chat.

Use it like this:

1. Tap the matching feature in Quick Actions to control the bed directly. You can also tap edit to change the Quick Actions shown there.

![Adjustable bed Quick Actions](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.24.14@2x.png)

2. If you want to adjust more bed functions, such as position, massage, or lighting, tap Remote in the bottom navigation bar.
3. If you want to view the settings for this bed, tap the settings button in the upper-right corner.

### 3.4 View Sleep Reports and History {#sleep-report}

If you want to review today's sleep results or look back at earlier sleep records, open Sleep Report here. In addition to the total score, you can also view more detail and trend changes for each metric.

#### 3.4.1 View a Sleep Report {#view-sleep-report}

1. On the Home page, tap the sleep score area, or pull down to open Sleep Report.

![Sleep Report entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%206.png)

2. On the report page, first review the current sleep results and each metric. Here, "today's" report usually refers to the sleep session from last night through this morning. The current report includes 3 categories and 9 metrics in total.

![Report overview](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.28.08@2x.png)

3. If you want to view data from another date, tap the current date first.

![Report date entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.22.40@2x%201.png)

4. Choose the date you want in the pop-up window to switch to the matching historical sleep report.

![Report date picker](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.28.46@2x.png)

5. Tap the score for a category to view the metrics under that category.

#### 3.4.2 View Metric Details and Trends {#view-metric-detail}

1. On the Sleep Report page, tap any metric card to open its detail page.

![Metric detail entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.22.40@2x%203.png)

2. On the metric detail page, review the more detailed explanation for that metric. If you want to keep asking questions, tap Ask me in the daily view.

![Metric detail page](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.29.59@2x.png)

3. You can switch between weekly, monthly, and yearly trends for that metric.

![Metric trend switcher](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.30.34@2x.png)

### 3.5 View Real-Time Data {#realtime-data}

If you want to check your current live body data, such as heart rate, respiratory rate, and movement, open Real-Time Data. This feature only appears in Quick Actions on the smart bed Home page.

1. On the Home page, tap Real-Time Data in Quick Actions.

![Real-Time Data entry](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/Real-Time%20Data.png)

2. Review the current values in the live metrics area. The current version mainly shows Heart Rate, Respiratory Rate, and Sleep Movement.

![Connected real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/RealTimeData-DoubleSize-Connected.png)

3. If your bed supports dual-sleeper mode, the page shows data for both Sleeper Left and Sleeper Right.

![Dual-sleeper real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.36.51@2x.png)

4. If the bed is not connected, real-time values will not appear. Return to the Home page, confirm the connection status, then open this page again.

![Disconnected real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/RealTimeData-Disconnected.png)

### 3.6 Use the Remote {#remote}

The Remote page is where you directly control common bed functions such as position adjustment, massage, preset modes, and lighting. Different bed models support different functions, so the page shows the buttons available for the bed you are currently connected to.

1. Open the Remote page from the bottom navigation bar.

![Remote entry](/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.40.26@2x.png)

2. If the bed is already connected, use the buttons shown on the page. Available controls may vary by bed model.

{{image-row|Bluetooth bed|/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.41.36@2x.png|Wi-Fi bed|/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.42.09@2x.png}}

3. If the page shows a disconnected state, return to the Home page, reconnect the bed, then come back to Remote.

### 3.7 Use Sleep Schedule {#sleep-schedule}

If you want the bed to adjust position, start massage, or turn on lighting automatically at a set time, use Sleep Schedule here. You can create a repeating weekly schedule or a one-time schedule for tomorrow only.

If a weekly schedule and a tomorrow-only schedule both exist, the system will prioritize the tomorrow-only schedule. The temporary schedule runs only once. After it runs, later dates go back to following the weekly schedule.

#### 3.7.1 Open Sleep Schedule {#open-sleep-schedule}

Open Sleep Schedule from Quick Actions.

![Sleep Schedule entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.43.11@2x.png)

#### 3.7.2 Use a Weekly Sleep Schedule {#weekly-sleep-schedule}

1. On the Sleep Schedule page, review the Active Schedule and Weekly Schedule sections.

![Weekly schedule overview](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.44.12@2x%201.png)

2. Tap Add New Schedule, then set the plan for each day of the week. You can give weekdays and weekends different routines, or set only some days.

![Add weekly schedule](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.46.07@2x%201.png)

3. On the edit page, set Rest Actions and Rise Actions, such as bed position, massage, or lighting, then save. The bed will repeat those actions at your chosen bedtime and wake-up time according to the weekly schedule.

![Weekly schedule action editor](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.46.07@2x%202.png)

#### 3.7.3 Set a Temporary Schedule for Tomorrow Only {#tomorrow-sleep-schedule}

1. If you only want to adjust tomorrow one time, tap Add for Tomorrow Only or Change for Tomorrow Only.

![Tomorrow-only schedule entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.44.12@2x%202.png)

2. On the edit page, set the Rest Actions and Rise Actions to run tomorrow only. After you save, the system will run that temporary schedule only tomorrow. If tomorrow has both a weekly schedule and a temporary schedule, the temporary one takes priority.

![Tomorrow-only schedule editor](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.45.14@2x.png)
