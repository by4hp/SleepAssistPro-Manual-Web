# 3. Using the App {#using-app}

Connection and first-time setup done? Now it's time to get familiar with the app. This chapter covers the home screen, Sleep Report, Real-Time Data, Remote, and Sleep Schedule.

### 3.1 Sign In {#login}

Smart bed users can sign in here to reach the home screen. Adjustable beds usually don't need an account — just connect and go.

#### 3.1.1 Sign In with Email {#login-email}

1. Open the Sign In page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Tap Sign In.

![Sign In page](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161252.png)

4. Sign-in successful — you're on the home screen.

![Home screen](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Homepage-Full.png)

#### 3.1.2 Sign In with Apple {#login-apple}

Signed up with Apple? Tap the Apple option on the Sign In page.

1. Tap Apple at the bottom of the Sign In page.

![Apple sign-in entry](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161306.png)

2. Complete Apple authorization on the system screen.

![Apple authorization](/manual/chapter-03/login/sign-up-apple-authorization.png)

#### 3.1.3 Sign In with Google {#login-google}

Signed up with Google? Tap the Google option on the Sign In page.

1. Tap Google at the bottom of the Sign In page.

![Google sign-in entry](/manual/chapter-03/login/Pasted%20image%2020260325161315.png)

2. Complete Google authorization on the system screen. (Image missing)

![Google authorization](/manual/chapter-03/login/sign-in-google-authorization-placeholder.svg)

### 3.2 Smart Bed Home {#homepage-smart}

The smart bed home screen brings together everything you use most: sleep reports, key metrics, quick actions, and settings.

First time here? Walk through it in this order:

#### 3.2.1 Sleep Score {#homepage-score}

1. When the selected date has sleep data, the top of the page shows your sleep score and summary.

![Smart bed home](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Homepage-Full.png)

2. The page auto-refreshes once on load. To get the latest data manually, tap the refresh button.

![Pull to refresh](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.14.12@2x.png)

3. To view another date, tap the date and switch using the popup picker.

{{image-row|Date entry|/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.14.44@2x%201.png|Date picker|/manual/chapter-03/homepage/Calendar.png}}

#### 3.2.2 Your Highlights {#homepage-highlights}

4. Your Highlights shows key sleep metric cards — swipe left or right to browse.

![Highlights cards](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x.png)

5. To adjust what's shown, tap Edit on the right.

![Highlights edit entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%201.png)

6. The home screen shows the top three cards. Drag to reorder, then tap the button in the upper right to save.

![Highlights order edit](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.17.30@2x.png)

#### 3.2.3 Quick Actions {#homepage-quick-actions}

7. Quick Actions gives you fast access to features like Real-Time Data, Sleep Schedule, and more.

![Quick Actions area](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%202.png)

8. To adjust order or content, tap Edit on the right.

![Quick Actions edit entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%203.png)

9. Same idea — the home screen shows the top four actions. Drag to reorder, then save.

![Quick Actions order edit](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.20.48@2x.png)

#### 3.2.4 Other {#homepage-other}

10. For a more detailed sleep analysis, tap or pull down the sleep score area to open Sleep Report.

![Sleep Report entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%204.png)

11. Need to change account, notification, device, or other settings? Tap Settings.

![Settings entry](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%205.png)

### 3.3 Adjustable Bed Home {#homepage-electric}

The adjustable bed home screen is all about quick control: positions, massage, lighting, and shortcuts. You won't see account features, Sleep Report, Real-Time Data, or AI Chat here.

1. Tap a feature in Quick Actions to control the bed directly. You can also tap edit to customize what's shown.

![Adjustable bed Quick Actions](/manual/chapter-03/homepage/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.24.14@2x.png)

2. For more controls (positions, massage, lighting, etc.), tap Remote in the bottom navigation.
3. To view this bed's settings, tap the settings button in the upper right.

### 3.4 View Sleep Reports & History {#sleep-report}

Want to see today's sleep results or look back at previous nights? Open Sleep Report. Beyond the total score, you can drill into each metric's details and trends.

#### 3.4.1 View a Sleep Report {#view-sleep-report}

1. On the home screen, tap the sleep score area or pull down to open Sleep Report.

![Sleep Report entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.16.40@2x%206.png)

2. Start with today's sleep results and metrics. "Today" usually means the sleep session from last night through this morning. The current report covers 3 categories with 9 metrics total.

![Report overview](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.28.08@2x.png)

3. To view another date, tap the current date.

![Report date entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.22.40@2x%201.png)

4. Pick the date you want from the popup to switch to that night's report.

![Report date picker](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-25%20at%2016.28.46@2x.png)

5. Tap a category score to view the metrics under that category.

#### 3.4.2 View Metric Details & Trends {#view-metric-detail}

1. In Sleep Report, tap any metric card to open its detail page.

![Metric detail entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.22.40@2x%203.png)

2. Check the detailed data explanation. To ask follow-up questions, tap Ask me in the daily view.

![Metric detail page](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.29.59@2x.png)

3. Switch between weekly, monthly, and yearly trends.

![Metric trend switcher](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-report/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.30.34@2x.png)

### 3.5 View Real-Time Data {#realtime-data}

Want to see your live body data — heart rate, respiratory rate, body movement? Open Real-Time Data. This feature only appears in Quick Actions on the smart bed home screen.

1. In Quick Actions on the home screen, tap Real-Time Data.

![Real-Time Data entry](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/Real-Time%20Data.png)

2. Check the current readings in the live metrics area. The current version shows Heart Rate, Respiratory Rate, and Sleep Movement.

![Connected real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/RealTimeData-DoubleSize-Connected.png)

3. If your bed supports dual-sleeper mode, the page shows data for both Sleeper Left and Sleeper Right.

![Dual-sleeper real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.36.51@2x.png)

4. If it shows disconnected, there won't be any live data. Go back to the home screen, check the connection, and try again.

![Disconnected real-time data](/manual/chapter-03/realtime-data/RealTimeData-Disconnected.png)

### 3.6 Use the Remote {#remote}

The Remote page lets you directly control your bed: positions, massage, presets, and lighting. Available controls vary by bed model — the page shows what's available for your current bed.

1. Open Remote from the bottom navigation.

![Remote entry](/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.40.26@2x.png)

2. If connected, just use the buttons on screen. Available controls may differ by bed model.

{{image-row|Bluetooth bed|/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.41.36@2x.png|Wi-Fi bed|/manual/chapter-03/remote/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.42.09@2x.png}}

3. If it shows disconnected, go back to the home screen, reconnect the bed, then return to Remote.

### 3.7 Use Sleep Schedule {#sleep-schedule}

Want the bed to automatically adjust positions, start massage, or turn on lighting at set times? Set up a Sleep Schedule. You can create a repeating weekly schedule or a one-time schedule for tomorrow only.

If both a weekly schedule and a tomorrow-only schedule exist, the system runs the tomorrow-only one first. The temporary schedule runs once, then future dates go back to the weekly plan.

#### 3.7.1 Open Sleep Schedule {#open-sleep-schedule}

Open Sleep Schedule from Quick Actions.

![Sleep Schedule entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.43.11@2x.png)

#### 3.7.2 Use a Weekly Sleep Schedule {#weekly-sleep-schedule}

1. On the Sleep Schedule page, review the Active Schedule and Weekly Schedule sections.

![Weekly schedule overview](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.44.12@2x%201.png)

2. Tap Add New Schedule, then set the plan by day of the week. Weekdays and weekends can have different routines, or you can set only certain days.

![Add weekly schedule](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.46.07@2x%201.png)

3. On the edit page, set Rest Actions and Rise Actions (positions, massage, lighting, etc.), then save. The bed will repeat these actions at your chosen bedtime and wake-up time.

![Weekly schedule actions](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.46.07@2x%202.png)

#### 3.7.3 Set a One-Time Schedule for Tomorrow Only {#tomorrow-sleep-schedule}

1. Just want to change tomorrow? Tap Add for Tomorrow Only or Change for Tomorrow Only.

![Tomorrow-only entry](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.44.12@2x%202.png)

2. Set the Rest Actions and Rise Actions for tomorrow, then save. The system runs this one-time schedule tomorrow only. If there's also a weekly schedule, the one-time plan takes priority.

![Tomorrow-only editor](/manual/chapter-03/sleep-schedule/CleanShot%202026-03-26%20at%2008.45.14@2x.png)
