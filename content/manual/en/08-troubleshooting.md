# 8. Troubleshooting {#troubleshooting}

Running into issues with connecting, signing in, viewing reports, or using AI? Find the section that matches your situation — it's usually the fastest way to a fix.

### 8.1 Can't Find the Bed or Connection Fails {#connection-issues}

App can't find your bed, or the connection gets stuck? Check these in order:

#### 8.1.1 Check the Basics First {#check-connection-conditions}

1. Make sure the bed is powered on and your phone is nearby.
2. If connecting a Wi-Fi smart bed, confirm you're using a dedicated 2.4G or 5G band — not a combined multi-band network.
3. Restart the bed search and try again.

#### 8.1.2 Auto-Connection Failed {#auto-identify-failed}

1. Auto-search didn't find it? Try Scan QR Code with Camera first.
2. QR code didn't work? Switch to manual device ID entry.
3. Bed found but type can't be confirmed? Select the correct type manually and continue.

Still can't connect? See {{jump-link|8.2 Permissions Not Enabled|troubleshooting|permissions}}.

### 8.2 Permissions Not Enabled {#permissions}

The first time you connect, the app may ask for Bluetooth, location, and camera permissions.

- Bluetooth affects device search and connection.
- Location affects nearby device scanning.
- Camera affects QR code scanning.

Without these, the app may not find the bed, can't scan QR codes, or can't continue connecting. Go to your phone's system settings, grant the missing permissions, then come back and try again.

### 8.3 Wi-Fi Smart Bed Connection Fails {#wifi-connection}

Wi-Fi smart bed stuck on the Wi-Fi or server connection step? Work through these:

1. Confirm the Wi-Fi network is correct and not a combined multi-band network.
2. Double-check the Wi-Fi password.
3. Keep your phone near the bed while the app completes Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and server connections.
4. If the page doesn't change for a while, go back, reselect the Wi-Fi, and try again.
5. Still not working? Start the bed search and setup over from scratch.

### 8.4 Home Screen Shows Disconnected {#disconnected}

If the home screen shows disconnected, the app isn't currently connected to your bed.

1. Look for the disconnected prompt and the Proceed to bed connection link.
2. Tap Proceed to bed connection.
3. Follow the prompts to reconnect.
4. If search or identification issues come up again, check {{jump-link|8.1 Can't Find the Bed or Connection Fails|troubleshooting|connection-issues}} and {{jump-link|8.2 Permissions Not Enabled|troubleshooting|permissions}}.

### 8.5 Home Screen Shows No Report {#no-report}

If the home screen shows No Report, there's no sleep report available for the selected date. This only appears for smart beds.

1. Confirm the home screen is showing No Report.
2. Tap the date to switch to another day and see if data is available.
3. Make sure your account and bed are connected normally.
4. If you just finished a sleep session today, wait for the data to generate, then check again.

![Home screen No Report](/manual/chapter-08/homepage/No%20report.png)

Data still not showing? Check the bed connection status once more.

### 8.6 AI Content or Real-Time Data Temporarily Unavailable {#ai-data-unavailable}

AI Chat not showing what you expected, or Real-Time Data has no values? Check these:

- Is there enough sleep data for the current date?
- Is the app connected to the bed right now?
- Is the content still loading?

Make sure the home screen shows the bed as connected and the selected date has valid data, then reopen the page.

### 8.7 Verification Code Not Arriving or Can't Sign In {#login-issues}

Stuck during sign-up, sign-in, or password reset? Check these:

1. Is the email address correct?
2. Give the system email a moment to send, then check your inbox.
3. If signing in to an existing account, make sure you're using the right method (email, Apple, or Google).
4. Still can't get in? Tap Forgot Password? on the Sign In page and follow the email to reset.

Already signed in and just want to change your password? Go to {{jump-link|6.4 Change or Recover Your Password|account-security|password}}.
