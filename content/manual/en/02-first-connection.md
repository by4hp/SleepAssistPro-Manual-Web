# 2. First Connection & Setup {#first-connection}

This chapter walks you through connecting your bed to the app and finishing the basic setup. If it's your first time, just follow the steps in order.

### 2.1 Before You Start {#preparation}

Take half a minute to check the following — it'll make the rest of the connection much smoother:

- The bed is powered on and ready to connect
- Your phone is close to the bed so the app can search nearby
- Bluetooth, location, and camera permissions are enabled on your phone
- If connecting a Wi-Fi smart bed, have the Wi-Fi name and password ready. The bed only supports a dedicated 2.4G or 5G band — combined multi-band networks won't work
- If auto-search doesn't find your bed, try scanning the QR code first; if the bed is found but the type is wrong, select the correct type manually; if scanning doesn't work either, enter the device ID manually

All set? Move on to {{jump-link|2.2 Start Connecting Your Bed|first-connection|start-connection}}.

### 2.2 Start Connecting Your Bed {#start-connection}

Once you're ready, start from the welcome screen.

The first time you open the app, you'll see a welcome screen with two main buttons: Connect Your Bed and No Bed Yet?

1. Already have a bed — tap Connect Your Bed.
2. Don't have a bed yet — tap No Bed Yet? to visit the Ergomotion website and learn more.

![Welcome screen](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/01-Entry-And-Setup/entry-welcome.png)

Started connecting? Next up: {{jump-link|2.3 Find and Connect Your Bed|first-connection|find-identify-bed}}.

### 2.3 Find and Connect Your Bed {#find-identify-bed}

Start with the easiest option — automatic connection. In most cases the app will find your bed right away. If it doesn't, try the QR code. If that doesn't work either, enter the device ID manually. If the bed is found but the type can't be confirmed, select the correct type on screen. Once the bed type is confirmed, continue to {{jump-link|2.4 Continue Setup by Bed Type|first-connection|bed-type-setup}}.

#### 2.3.1 Automatic Connection {#auto-identify}

The easiest way — most users get connected right here.

1. Tap Connect to start searching for your bed.

![Start searching](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/01-Entry-And-Setup/entry-bed-setup-instructions.png)

2. If a Bluetooth permission prompt appears, tap Allow — otherwise the app can't find nearby beds.

![Bluetooth permission prompt](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/02-Bed-Search-And-Identification/bed-search-bluetooth-permission-required.png)

3. Wait while the app searches for nearby beds.

![Searching](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/02-Bed-Search-And-Identification/bed-search-loading.png)

4. When beds are found, a list appears. Select yours and continue.

![Search results](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/02-Bed-Search-And-Identification/bed-search-results-multiple-beds.png)

5. If the app finds your bed but can't determine the type yet, select it first, then follow the prompts to the bed type selection page.

![Device found but type not identified](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/02-Bed-Search-And-Identification/bed-selection-before-type-identification.png)

6. Confirm the correct bed type on the selection page, then continue.

![Bed type selection](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/02-Bed-Search-And-Identification/bed-identification-type-selection.png)

#### 2.3.2 QR Code Connection {#qr-identify}

Auto-search didn't find your bed? Try scanning the QR code instead.

1. On the Unable to Connect page, tap Scan QR Code with Camera.

![Unable to connect page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/alternative-connection-unable-to-connect.png)

2. If a camera permission prompt appears, tap Allow.

![Camera permission prompt](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/qr-scan-camera-permission-required.png)

3. Point your camera at the QR code on the control box underneath the bed.

![QR scan page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/qr-scan-overview.png)

4. Once connected, follow the on-screen prompts to continue.

5. If it says connection failed, make sure Bluetooth is on, then go back and try again.

![Connection failed — Bluetooth off](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/Unable%20to%20Connect%20-%20Retry%20Options.png)

#### 2.3.3 Manual Device ID Entry {#manual-identify}

QR code not working, or still can't connect after scanning? Enter the device ID manually.

1. On the Unable to Connect page, tap Manually Enter Bluetooth ID.
2. The device ID is on the control box underneath the bed — see the illustration for reference.

![Unable to connect page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/alternative-connection-unable-to-connect.png)

3. Enter your smart bed's device ID.

![Bluetooth ID entry page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/manual-bluetooth-id-entry.png)

4. Make sure the entry looks right, then continue. If the format is wrong, the page will tell you — just fix it and try again.

![Input error prompt](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/manual-bluetooth-id-error.png)

5. Once connected, follow the on-screen prompts to continue.

6. If it says connection failed, make sure Bluetooth is on, then go back and try again.

![Connection failed — Bluetooth off](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/03-Alternative-Connection/Unable%20to%20Connect%20-%20Retry%20Options.png)

#### 2.3.4 Not Ready to Connect Yet {#skip-connect}

Don't want to connect right now, or this bed was already connected before? Tap Skip To Sign In to move on.

![Bed connection instructions](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/01-Entry-And-Setup/entry-bed-setup-instructions.png)

If this is your first time, or the bed hasn't been linked to your account yet, go back to the earlier sections above.

### 2.4 Continue Setup by Bed Type {#bed-type-setup}

After your bed is found, what comes next depends on the bed type. Adjustable beds go straight to the home screen. Smart beds need account sign-up or sign-in first, then more setup.

#### 2.4.1 Adjustable Bed {#electric-bed-setup}

Adjustable beds don't need an account — the app goes straight to the Homepage once connected, and you're ready to go. Next, check out {{jump-link|Adjustable Bed Home|using-app|homepage-electric}} and {{jump-link|Remote|using-app|remote}} in Chapter 3.

![Adjustable bed home](/manual/chapter-02/homepage/electric-bed-homepage.png)

#### 2.4.2 Smart Bed {#smart-bed-setup}

First-time smart bed users still need to sign up, sign in, and complete the initial profile. Next step: {{jump-link|2.5 Create Your Smart Bed Account|first-connection|register-account}}.

### 2.5 Create Your Smart Bed Account {#register-account}

First time with a smart bed and no account yet? Create one here. You can sign up with email, Apple, or Google. Once you've signed up and verified, continue to {{jump-link|2.6 Set Up Your Smart Bed|first-connection|setup-smart-bed}}.

#### 2.5.1 Sign Up with Email {#register-email}

Complete sign-up directly on the Sign Up page.

1. Enter your email address.

![Empty sign-up form](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-empty-form.png)

2. Create a password, then enter it again to confirm.

![Email and password entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-email-entered.png)

3. Select your region.

![Region selection](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-region-selection.png)

4. Check the required agreement boxes.

![Agreements checked](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-agreement-checked.png)

5. Tap the sign-up button.

![Sign-up submit](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-submit-clean-shot.png)

6. Check your email for the verification code, then enter it in the app to complete verification.

![Email verification](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/email-verification-clean-shot.png)

#### 2.5.2 Sign Up with Apple {#register-apple}

Already signed in to Apple on your phone? You can use it for a quick sign-up.

1. Tap Apple at the bottom of the sign-up page.

![Apple entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-submit-clean-shot.png)

2. Complete Apple authorization on the system screen.

![Apple authorization](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-apple-authorization.png)

3. Once done, you'll return to the app to continue setup.

#### 2.5.3 Sign Up with Google {#register-google}

Already signed in to Google on your phone? You can also use it for a quick sign-up.

1. Tap Google at the bottom of the sign-up page.

![Sign-up page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-up-empty-form.png)

2. Complete Google authorization on the system screen. (Image missing)

![Google authorization](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/04-Account-Registration-And-Verification/sign-in-google-authorization-placeholder.svg)

3. Once done, you'll return to the app to continue setup.

### 2.6 Set Up Your Smart Bed {#setup-smart-bed}

Account ready? Now finish connecting the smart bed. Bluetooth and Wi-Fi beds have slightly different steps, and you'll also need to confirm whether to turn on Sleep Data Tracking. Once that's done, continue to {{jump-link|2.7 Complete Your First Profile|first-connection|first-profile}}.

#### 2.6.1 Set Up a Bluetooth Smart Bed {#setup-bt-bed}

Bluetooth smart beds have a simpler process.

1. Wait for the app to finish the Bluetooth connection.

![Bluetooth connection stage](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/bluetooth-connection-stage-bluetooth.png)

2. Wait for the app to finish smart bed setup.

![Bed setup stage](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/bluetooth-connection-stage-bed-setup.png)

3. Connection complete — on to Sleep Data Tracking.

![Bluetooth connection complete](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/bluetooth-connection-complete.png)

#### 2.6.2 Set Up a Wi-Fi Smart Bed {#setup-wifi-bed}

Wi-Fi smart beds also need to be connected to your home Wi-Fi.

1. Select an available Wi-Fi network. Use a dedicated 2.4G or 5G band — don't use a combined multi-band network.

![Wi-Fi selection](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-network-selection.png)

2. Enter the Wi-Fi password and continue.

![Wi-Fi password entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-password-entry-empty.png)

3. Wait for the app to finish the Bluetooth connection.

![Bluetooth connection stage](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-connection-stage-bluetooth.png)

4. Wait for the app to finish the Wi-Fi connection.

![Wi-Fi connection stage](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-connection-stage-wifi.png)

5. Wait for the app to finish the server connection.

![Server connection stage](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-connection-stage-server.png)

6. Connection complete — on to Sleep Data Tracking.

![Wi-Fi connection complete](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/05-Smart-Bed-Connection/wifi-connection-complete.png)

#### 2.6.3 Turn On Sleep Data Tracking {#setup-sleep-tracking}

Once the bed is connected, the page shows Sleep Data Tracking, where you decide whether the app should record your sleep data going forward.

1. Read the instructions on the page.

![Sleep Data Tracking overview](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/06-Sleep-Data-Tracking/sleep-data-tracking-overview.png)

2. Choose whether to turn it on. If you turn it off, the app won't track sleep data or generate sleep reports. You can always turn it back on later in Settings.

![Turn-off confirmation](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/06-Sleep-Data-Tracking/sleep-data-tracking-turn-off-confirmation.png)

3. Tap Continue to move on to your first profile setup.

![Nickname entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/01-Shared-Steps/profile-name-entry-empty.png)

### 2.7 Complete Your First Profile {#first-profile}

Bed connected and Sleep Data Tracking confirmed — now fill in some basic info. You'll go through your nickname, basic details, sleep goals, and personal preferences. If your bed has split-zone features, there's one extra step to pick your sleeping side. Once you're done, you'll land on the home screen — head over to {{jump-link|3.2 Smart Bed Home|using-app|homepage-smart}}.

#### 2.7.1 Smart Bed Without Split-Zone Features {#profile-no-zone}

1. Enter your nickname.

![Nickname entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/01-Shared-Steps/profile-name-entry-filled.png)

2. Read the setup instructions on the page.

![Profile setup introduction](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/01-Shared-Steps/profile-setup-introduction.png)

3. Fill in basic info: gender, height, weight, mattress thickness, birthday, time format, etc.

![Basic info form](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/02-Without-Partition-Feature/sleep-profile-details-form.png)

4. After basic info, move on to the Sleep Goal page.

![Sleep Goal page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/02-Without-Partition-Feature/sleep-goals-overview.png)

5. Continue to the personal tags page and pick the ones that fit. You can also tap Skip for now.

![Personal tags](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/02-Without-Partition-Feature/sleep-preferences-selection-state.png)

6. Done — you'll enter the home screen.

#### 2.7.2 Smart Bed With Split-Zone Features {#profile-zone}

1. Enter your nickname.

![Nickname entry](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/01-Shared-Steps/profile-name-entry-filled.png)

2. Read the setup instructions on the page.

![Profile setup introduction](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/01-Shared-Steps/profile-setup-introduction.png)

3. First, choose your sleeping side.

![Bed side selection](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/03-With-Partition-Feature/profile-bed-side-selection.png)

4. Open the basic info page and confirm unit settings first.

![Unit selection](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/03-With-Partition-Feature/sleep-profile-units-selection.png)

If you adjust the units or a key field, the system may briefly save the settings.

![Unit configuration](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/03-With-Partition-Feature/sleep-profile-units-configuring.png)

5. After basic info, continue with Sleep Goal and choose the sleep area you want to improve first.

![Sleep Goal priority](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/03-With-Partition-Feature/sleep-goals-priority-selection.png)

6. On the final personal tags page, review everything and tap Finish to enter the home screen.

![Final page](/manual/chapter-02/onboarding/07-Profile-And-Preferences/03-With-Partition-Feature/sleep-preferences-final-state.png)

Once you see the home screen, your first connection and setup are all done.

![Settings home](/manual/chapter-02/settings/sleeper-tags-overview.png)
